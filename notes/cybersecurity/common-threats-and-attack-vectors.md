Learning Objectives
-------------------
- Identify and describe common types of malware (Ransomware, Spyware, Viruses).
- Recognize the tactics of social engineering, especially phishing.
- Understand the mechanics of top web application attacks like SQL Injection (SQLi) and Cross-Site Scripting (XSS).
- Describe network-level attacks like Denial-of-Service (DoS/DDoS) and Man-in-the-Middle (MitM).
