1. Sprint is an incremental release of software typically 1 - 3 weeks long
2. Sprint: Plan -> Build -> Test -> Review
3. Scrum roles: Product Owner, Scrum Master, Team
4. Scrum documents or artefacts: Product Backlog, Sprint Backlog, Burndown Chart
5. Scrum ceremonies: Sprint planning, Daily Scrum, Sprint Review
5. Product Backlog -> Sprint Planning -> Sprint Backlog -> Sprint -> Potentially Shippable Product -> Sprint Review and Retrospective
6. Three Pillars of Scrum: Transparency, Inspection and Adaptation
7. Scrum values: Courage, Focus, Commitment, Respect and Openness
8. `Stakeholders` are other members involved in the project other than the Scrum Team.
9. A user story is a description of a feature from the user's point of view
10. An Epic is a collection of closely related user stories
11. Story points are a unit-less measure of the amount of work needed to complete a user story
12. INVEST: Independent Negotiable Valuable Estimable Small Testable
