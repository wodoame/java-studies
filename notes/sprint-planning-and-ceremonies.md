Product Estimation
1. Planning Poker technique for product estimation

Daily Stand-up: 
Round Robin daily stand up involves going around asking people questions(what did you achieve, what will you be aiming to achieve, what are the blockers you will be facing today)
Walking the board uses a Kanban board. 

Sprint Retrospective:
A sprint retrospective is the last stage of a sprint
A sprint review is where the scrum team demonstrates what they have achieved
It's usually facilitated by the scrum master
sprint retrospective is about reviewing the process whilst sprint review is about demoing the work
